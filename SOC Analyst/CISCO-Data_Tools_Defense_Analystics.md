The Department of Information and Communications Technology (DICT) MIMAROPA, in partnership with Cisco Skills for All, brings a FREE, self-paced online courses.

What You'll Learn 

Through the Wonderland SOC environment, you'll gain experience with: 

Critical defense systems and analysis tools used in modern SOCs for both on-premises and cloud environments 

Threat intelligence sources and how to search for Indicators of Compromise (IOCs) 

Splunk Enterprise Security fundamentals, including the Common Information Model (CIM) and Data Models 

Essential Splunk commands like TSTATS, TRANSACTION, REX, EVAL, and LOOKUP for efficient threat investigations 

Best practices for composing powerful security searches that deliver results fast
