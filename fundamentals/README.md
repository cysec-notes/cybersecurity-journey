# Fundamentals Overview
This section documents my journey in learning the fundamentals of cybersecurity.

But first of all, let me introduce myself. I am currently studying Computer Engineering at a well-known university in our city. We have learned basic programming languages, building applications, creating websites with databases, and projects that use both hardware and software. But throughout the learning journey, we had a subject that introduced us to Kali Linux and operating systems, and that is when cybersecurity was introduced as well.
I have zero knowledge when it comes to how cybersecurity works in real-world scenarios. My teacher encouraged us to gain skills in this field, and I became curious. I did some research, and penetration testing was the first thing to catch my interest. I wanted to try hacking in my own virtual machine. However, after learning from cybersecurity professionals from the internet, I realized that building a strong foundation is essential before moving into hands-on hacking and security labs. It will help me develop the knowledge and mindset needed to work effectively in cybersecurity. Learning these foundations can provide me with an introduction to key concepts, tools, operating systems, networking, and security principles that I will use throughout my learning journey.

Lastly, the materials in this directory come from multiple sources, including Hack The Box, classroom activities and laboratories, online courses, and other free educational resources. Together, they provide the foundational knowledge needed to support my growth in both Blue Team and Red Team cybersecurity practices.
