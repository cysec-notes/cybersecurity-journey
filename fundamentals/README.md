# Fundamentals Overview
This section documents my journey in learning the foundations of cybersecurity.

When most people think about cybersecurity, they often focus on hacking techniques and penetration testing. That was my initial interest as well. However, after learning from cybersecurity professionals, instructors, and industry resources, I realized that building a strong foundation is essential before moving into hands-on hacking and security labs.

Understanding the fundamentals helps me develop the knowledge and mindset needed to work effectively in cybersecurity. It provides an introduction to key concepts, tools, operating systems, networking, and security principles that I will use throughout my learning journey.

The materials in this directory come from multiple sources, including Hack The Box, classroom activities and laboratories, online courses, and other free educational resources. Together, they provide the foundational knowledge needed to support my growth in both Blue Team and Red Team cybersecurity practices.
